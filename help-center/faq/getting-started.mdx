## Overview

To start using Gobind Bakhshi:

1. Sign up at `https://gobindbakhshi.com/register`
2. Confirm your email address
3. Log in to your dashboard

## Create Your First Project

1. Click "New Project" in the top menu
2. Add a project name and description
3. Save to begin organizing your documentation

You're ready to manage your docs!
