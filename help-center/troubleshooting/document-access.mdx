---
title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: What to do if you can't access your project docs.
---

## Symptoms

- Error: "Access denied" when opening docs
- Documents not visible in project list

## Possible Causes

- Not logged in
- Permissions not set for your account

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify login">
    ````
    Ensure you are signed in at https://gobindbakhshi.com/login
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    ````
    Ask the project admin to grant you access under Project Settings > Members
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
