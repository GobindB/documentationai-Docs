## Sign Up for an Account

Create your Gobind Bakhshi account in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.gobindbakhshi.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a strong password. Accept the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New Documentation Space

Organize your project docs in a dedicated space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Documentation Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and choose a slug (e.g., `my-project-docs`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Visibility" icon="eye">
    Choose public, private, or team access. Add a description for your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create** to generate your space at `https://gobindbakhshi.com/docs/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
